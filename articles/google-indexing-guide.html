Google indexing time varies depending on domain authority, crawl priorities, internal linking, and content signals.

Does Google Require Minimum Quality?

There is no fixed ‚Äúminimum word count‚Äù or official quality threshold. However, Google must determine that a page is worth indexing.

If a page is thin, duplicate, or lacks internal links, Google may crawl it but not add it to the index.

Why Your Page Is Not Getting Indexed

  • Noindex meta tag present
  • Blocked by robots.txt
  • Incorrect canonical tag
  • Orphan page (no internal links)
  • Duplicate content
  • Very low site authority
  • Slow hosting or crawl errors

How To Get Indexed Faster

1. Use Google Search Console

2. Submit XML Sitemap

Ensure your sitemap.xml is submitted and updated regularly.

4. Increase Content Value

Add original insights, structured headings, FAQs, and real data.

5. Build Authority

Backlinks and consistent publishing improve crawl frequency.

Indexing vs Ranking

Being indexed does not guarantee ranking. Ranking depends on competition, content depth, authority, and search intent alignment.

Final SEO Advice

If your site is not getting indexed, focus on:

  • Publishing consistent content
  • Improving site speed
