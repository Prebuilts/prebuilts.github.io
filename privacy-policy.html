Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 12.02.2026

1. Introduction

Prebuilts.eu ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email address (when registering or using Google login)
  • Basic account information provided through Google authentication
  • Usage data such as page visits and interaction metrics
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type, and IP address

3. Google Authentication

We use Google authentication services for account login. Passwords and authentication credentials are securely handled and managed by Google. We do not store raw passwords on our servers. Google’s own privacy policy applies to authentication data processed by Google.

4. Data Usage

We use collected data solely for:

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Account management and authentication
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Website analytics and performance optimization

5. Advertising & Cookies

Our website may use third-party advertising services. These services may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to display relevant advertisements.

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help improve user experience and analyze website traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. No Selling of Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third-party companies. We do not share user data with advertising companies for personalized targeting beyond standard third-party ad network functionality.

7. AI & Data Usage Statement

We do not use user data to train artificial intelligence models. User data is not used for machine learning model development or external data analysis beyond operational functionality.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. Authentication data handled via Google services is secured according to Google’s infrastructure standards.

9. User Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal data, request corrections, or request deletion of your account. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us by sending us an email (Address not currently specified).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
